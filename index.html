IoT Project Summary

IoT-Based Smart City with Multi-Source Energy Harvesting Systems

The IoT-Based Smart City with Multi-Source Energy Harvesting Systems project integrates renewable energy, IoT, and automation to create sustainable smart city solutions. It includes solar street lights, piezo energy harvesting, wireless car charging, automatic rain detection, solar tree lighting, and automatic garden watering systems.

Project Objectives
1
To integrate solar, piezoelectric, and wireless energy sources into a unified harvesting framework.
2
To automate urban systems using IoT sensors for lighting and rain detection.
3
To evaluate system performance to validate energy savings and reliability as a replicable city model.
